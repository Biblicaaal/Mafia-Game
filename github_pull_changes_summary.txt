High-level result:
The local work focused on the Desk Don prototype, especially the Family tab and Providence simulation. The biggest shift is that Providence now has a more playable economy loop: clean cash, dirty cash, businesses, heat, expenses, daily income, opening grace periods, and AI auto-management all interact in a more legible way.

1. Family tree and relationship tuning
- Blood and Mafia family trees were iterated to fix duplicated/repeated visible relationship values and overly high starting opinions.
- Opinion values were retuned so relationships generally start closer to a modest -20 / 0 / +20 range, leaving room for growth.
- Blood family opinions no longer use rank-distance logic.
- Blood family bonuses remain stronger than more distant social/cultural bonuses.
- Hidden motivations such as greed, jealousy, ambition, and related hidden attributes are treated as hidden or partially obscured rather than fully exposed as explicit player-visible motives.
- Blood family surname behavior was adjusted so the core bloodline coheres around a shared surname while spouses/outsiders can retain different names.
- Mafia family naming was expanded so the organization is not always "Moretti"; the Don/culture can drive family or organization names.
- Culture-specific organization framing was added or tuned, such as yakuza/cartel-style naming for non-Italian groups.
- Outsider/ethnic fit penalties were added conceptually: lower ranks may tolerate outsiders more, while higher ladder positions can impose harsher cultural/rank barriers.

2. Mafia tree UI behavior
- Mafia tree received pan/zoom behavior similar to the blood family tree.
- Text selection while panning was suppressed.
- Extra visible pan/zoom controls were removed per user preference.
- Mafia node click selection was restored after a regression.
- Mafia popovers were anchored beside the selected member like blood family popovers.
- Mafia popovers now dim the rest of the tree similarly to blood family selection focus.
- Popover dimming was corrected so the popover itself stays bright/readable.
- Panning with a selected character no longer closes the extended info; it should close on right click or a deliberate click-away instead.

3. Providence event framework and metrics
- Providence was expanded into an event/director framework with hidden metrics and event weighting.
- Metrics discussed and tuned include prosperity, heat, stability, ambition pressure, momentum, fortune, family opinion, and mafia opinion.
- Fortune was changed to drift once per day with a random signed movement instead of biasing upward every tick.
- Momentum and event pressure were retuned so events do not happen every single tick/day by default.
- Severity weighting was made more responsive to hidden metric ranges: high prosperity/heat/ambition can increase bad-event risk, while low stability/prosperity can increase recovery opportunities.
- Family Opinion and Mafia Opinion were added as aggregate relationship metrics toward the player.
- Event effects were extended so events can change clean cash, dirty cash, stress, health, reputation, opinions, and related variables.
- Stress/health/reputation interactions were added:
  - High stress can drain health.
  - Hospital action can heal health.
  - Low health can hurt prosperity/stability.
  - High reputation can support stability.
  - High heat can lower reputation.

4. Clean cash / dirty cash economy
- Money is now split into clean cash and dirty cash.
- Clean cash is needed for most normal purchases and daily ordinary expenses.
- Dirty cash is used for dirty rackets, heat expenses, corruption/lobbying, and funneling through fronts.
- Clean businesses now pay clean money only; they no longer generate dirty money.
- Clean businesses require dirty money as fuel/funnel input to generate clean income.
- Dirty rackets generate stronger dirty income but add heat pressure and create a need for clean fronts.
- Dirty business expenses are now paid with dirty money instead of draining clean cash.
- Clean/front/family/organization overhead is paid with clean money.
- Heat expenses are paid with dirty money.
- Daily values are shown explicitly as " / day" in the Providence UI.
- Cash balances are shown as stored totals, while income/expenses/net values are shown as daily numeric values.

5. Business system iterations
- Multiple copies of the same business can now be purchased.
- AI auto-buy was repeatedly tuned to avoid:
  - hoarding dirty cash forever,
  - buying only clean businesses,
  - buying only dirty rackets,
  - stopping expansion too early,
  - letting heat instantly bankrupt the player.
- Current clean businesses are tuned to produce more meaningful clean daily profit:
  - Laundry Route: clean income 260/day, expense 70/day, buy-in 1500, net +190/day before dirty fuel considerations.
  - Shipping Office: clean income 760/day, expense 210/day, buy-in 6200, net +550/day before dirty fuel considerations.
- Dirty rackets still provide the stronger high-risk dirty income lane:
  - Corner Book and Union Kickback remain stronger dirty earners and are used to fuel clean fronts.
- AI scoring now gives clean fronts more value when:
  - clean income is below clean need,
  - dirty cash is overflowing,
  - heat is becoming a problem,
  - the organization has too many rackets relative to fronts.

6. Expenses, heat, and opening pacing
- Heat expenses were initially too explosive and could bankrupt the player at/after day 60.
- A 60-day heat grace period was added:
  - Heat is locked during days 1-60.
  - Heat expenses are 0/day during the grace window.
  - Wealth attention is 0/day during the grace window.
- After day 60, heat pressure ramps in slowly until about day 105 instead of hitting all at once.
- Wealth attention was softened and delayed:
  - Hoarded cash still attracts attention.
  - Dirty hoards and racket-heavy portfolios matter.
  - The curve is much less punishing than earlier iterations.
- Opening clean overhead was also ramped down:
  - Days 1-20 start around 8% ordinary overhead.
  - Days 21-60 rise slowly.
  - After day 60, overhead ramps toward full cost gradually.
- This fixed the issue where clean expenses consumed all clean cash before the AI could afford even one clean business.
